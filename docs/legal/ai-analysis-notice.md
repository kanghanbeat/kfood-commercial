# AI Analysis Notice

Effective date: [Effective Date]

K-Food Travel may offer AI-assisted food photo analysis to help identify possible foods, ingredients, categories, or review signals.

## Reference Only

AI analysis is for reference only. Results may be inaccurate, incomplete, outdated, or inappropriate. Users should verify important information independently.

## Not Professional Advice

AI results do not provide medical, allergy, nutrition, legal, safety, or professional advice. Users with allergies, dietary restrictions, health conditions, or safety concerns should consult reliable sources or qualified professionals.

## Image Handling

Images or image URLs may be sent to a server-side AI analysis endpoint. Users should not upload sensitive images, private documents, payment information, medical information, images of children without appropriate consent, or photos of other people without permission.

## Reports And Improvements

Users can report inaccurate or inappropriate AI results through [Contact Email] or available in-app reporting tools. AI analysis features may be adjusted, improved, limited, or removed over time.

## Server-Side Key Safety

OpenAI or other AI provider API keys must remain server-side only and must not be included in Expo public environment variables or client-side source code.
